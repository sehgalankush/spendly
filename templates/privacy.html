Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and personalise your experience. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You can delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, all your personal data and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface. This means your browser makes a request to Google's servers when you visit the app. No personal or financial data is shared with Google through this process.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking scripts.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We take privacy seriously and will respond promptly.
